About BIST

BIST Bhopal, part of the Bansal Group of Institutes, is a premier technical and professional educational institute. The institute offers a wide range of engineering and management programs, with a focus on innovation, excellence, and holistic student development.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electronics & Communication
  • B.Tech in Electrical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
  • M.Tech in CSE, EC, Civil
  • MBA
  • Diploma Programs

Placement Highlights

  • Strong industry tie-ups and placement support
  • Top recruiters include TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Capgemini
  • Dedicated Training & Placement Cell
  • Internship opportunities for hands-on learning

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and classrooms
  • Library with thousands of books and e-resources
  • Wi-Fi enabled campus
  • Transport, Hostel, and Cafeteria facilities
